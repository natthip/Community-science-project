The Chemistry Lab

You get to perform exciting experiments and mix different chemicals. The chemistry lab is a highlight that will make you feel like a real chemist!

Journey to the Stars

As you step into the exhibition, the ambient lighting mimics the starlit night sky, setting the stage for an extraordinary experience. The journey begins with an exploration of the cosmic symphony the rhythmic dance of stars, the birth and demise of galaxies, and the interstellar melodies that resonate through the cosmos.

Dino Discovery

An exhibition that takes you back in time to the age of dinosaurs. Learn about their fascinating behaviors and even get a chance to dig for fossils. A prehistoric adventure that will make you feel like a paleontologist!

The Space Getaway

This exhibition will take you on an incredible adventure through the vastness of space. Explore the wonders of our galaxy, learn about our planets, and even experience what it's like to be an astronaut.

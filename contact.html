SHOP

Immerse yourself in a world of culture and creativity at the Museum Gift Shop. Located conveniently within the museum premises, it's the perfect way to extend your journey beyond the exhibits. Take a piece of history home and make your visit an enduring memory.

FOOD & DRINKS

Nestled amidst the cultural treasures of the museum, the Canteen is a haven where artistry extends beyond the canvas and into the kitchen. Our chefs draw inspiration from the exhibits, creating a menu that celebrates diversity, creativity.

ADMISSION

The entrance is free for all. There are guided tours of the museum that leave every hour. These tours are 70 NOK per person and include a handy printed guide of the museum. If you would like to organise a guided tour for your group of 6 or more people, please contact us to arrange the tour.

ACCESSIBILITY

The museum has wheelchair accessibility ramps. It also has audio guides and braille display signs for the visually impaired.
